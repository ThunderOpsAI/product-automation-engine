# SYSTEM 1: DIGITAL ARBITRAGE FACTORY — Overview

**What it does:** Autonomously creates and sells digital products on marketplaces  
**Revenue model:** $19–79 products on Gumroad/Etsy  
**Target:** $1,000–3,000/month by Month 2

---

## What "Autonomous" Actually Means

### Autonomous (Zero Human Input)
- Search marketplaces for trending niches
- Find public domain/licensed assets
- Enhance and format products
- Generate cover images
- Write product descriptions + SEO
- List on Gumroad/Etsy
- A/B test prices/titles/thumbnails
- Auto-respond to simple support ("How do I download?")
- Process refunds (<7 days, auto-approve)

### You Approve (30 min/day)
- Which 3 niches to build (once at start)
- Product quality check before first listing (confidence <8)
- Complex refund requests (>7 days, complaints)

### Goal
Wake up to:
- New products listed
- Sales notifications
- Support resolved automatically

---

## Money Flow

```
Acquisition (public domain assets) → Enhancement → Distribution → Optimization

Revenue streams:
- Low ticket: $9–19 (volume play)
- Mid ticket: $29–59 (main revenue)
- Bundle: $79–149 (upsell)

Target math:
- 10 SKUs × $19 avg × 6 sales/day = $3,420/mo gross
- After fees (30% Gumroad + refunds): ~$2,400/mo net
```

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Fix |
|------|-----|
| No sales after 30 days | Rotate niches fast, A/B pricing, add free samples |
| Platform suppression | Multi-platform from day 1, compliance checks |
| High refund rate | Better previews, stronger docs, quality threshold |
